Our Story

A Legacy of Healing & Care

Founded in 1999, Aetheria Global Hospital began as a small clinic with a big vision — to make world-class healthcare accessible to all. Over 25 years, we've grown into a 500-bed multi-specialty hospital serving over one million patients.

Today, we stand as a beacon of hope for patients from across the country and internationally, offering cutting-edge treatments, compassionate care, and a commitment to excellence in everything we do.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, high-quality, affordable healthcare services to every patient, every time, using the latest medical advancements while preserving the human touch in healing.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare institution in Asia, recognized globally for clinical excellence, patient-centered care, and pioneering medical research and education.

Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, and Respect. These values guide every decision we make and every interaction with our patients and their families.
